From Recipe to Reel: Sharing Andalusian Christmas Food with the World

LET’S GET STARTED!

Welcome to the project

Have you ever watched cooking videos on Instagram, TikTok or YouTube Shorts?

In this project, you will become content creators. You will learn how to explain an Andalusian Christmas recipe in English and share it with an international audience through a short reel video.

You will work in teams to:

- research a traditional recipe
- write a clear script
- practise speaking naturally
- record and edit your own reel

At the end of the project, you will present your video to the class.

Your challenge:
Can you make people from other countries understand and enjoy Andalusian food?

What will you learn?

During this project, you will learn...

  • To explain a recipe in English clearly and in an organized manner.
  • To improve their fluency and pronunciation when speaking without reading from a script.
  • To practice adapting cultural information (Andalusian cuisine) for people from other countries.
  • To create a short “Reel”-style video using digital tools.
  • To work as a team and organize a real-world project.

Final product

A short video (1–2 minutes) in which:
- You will present a traditional Andalusian recipe.
- You will explain the ingredients and steps.
- You will adapt the content for an international audience.
- You should pay attention to your pronunciation, fluency, and clarity.
- The final video can be shared in the classroom or on an educational platform.
